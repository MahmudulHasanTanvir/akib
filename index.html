About Ataur

Ataur Rahman Akib is a popular Bangladeshi singer, songwriter, music producer and music director based in NY, USA. His assiduous struggle is to disseminate the core message of unity, love and humanity across the globe. The journey he started from a small green village in Bangladesh around 18 years ago where an uninterruptable electricity was imaginary. Breaking all these barriers he came forward with his own determination and motivation to a level that his mom always insisted on these early days. However, he took a bold leadership of apex islamic cultural organizations in Dhaka, London in recent years; and currently working based on New York, USA. The daunting challenge for Iqbal HJ is to bridge the gap between east and west and provide an element of songs that can touch the heart of both portion of listeners, that he is believed to have accomplished, in some cases. The singer’s first international album released on 2016 titled ‘Make Me Your Friend’ hat widely lauded in home and abroad. In this album the ‘Labbaik Allah’, a song written by his friend Mohammed from Hijri, Group, is most viewed and shared on internet. The music video for this song was shot on location in Saudi Arabia, London UK, USA, Northern Ireland and Bangladesh.
